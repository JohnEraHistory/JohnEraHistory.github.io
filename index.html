Welcome to Stories of America’s Wars & Conflicts

The told and untold stories of WW2, the Cold War, and America’s other conflicts. Discover the people involved, the decisions made, the secret missions, and understand what really happened and why.

Time Periods Covered

  • WW2: 1939-1945
  • Cold War: 1947-1991
  • Korean War: 1950-1953
  • Vietnam War: 1955-1975
  • Iraq Gulf War: 1990-1991
  • Iraq Freedom: 2003-2011
  • Enduring Freedom: 2001-2014

About This Site

The articles presented here are curated from various sources including the internet, books, and magazines. As an 89-year-old USAF veteran (1954-1975), this is a labor of love and a cherished hobby. My grandson suggested establishing this website and has been instrumental in bringing it to life. New articles will be added each week; I hope you find them engaging and informative.

How to Access Articles

  1. Scroll down to the green block labeled “Go to Articles”.
  2. Choose an article, then click on “PDF” to view or download.
